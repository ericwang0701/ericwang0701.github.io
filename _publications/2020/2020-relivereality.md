abstract: >-
  We present ReliveReality, a novel experience-sharing method that transforms traditional
  photo and video memories into socially relivable 3D experiences in virtual reality.
  ReliveReality leverages deep learning-based computer vision techniques to reconstruct clothed
  humans, estimate multi-person 3D pose, and recover 3D environments using only a single RGB
  camera. Integrated into a networked multi-user VR environment, ReliveReality allows multiple
  users to enter a reconstructed past experience together, freely navigate it, and relive
  memories from different perspectives. We describe the system’s technical implementation and
  discuss broader implications of reconstructing and sharing personal experiences in VR,
  particularly with respect to privacy.
