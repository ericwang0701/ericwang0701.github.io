abstract: >-
  Mixed Reality (MR) systems can support user tasks by integrating virtual content into the physical
  environment, but determining where and how to place this content remains challenging due to the
  dynamic nature of MR experiences. In contrast to prior optimization-based approaches, we explore
  how reinforcement learning (RL) can support continuous 3D content placement that adapts to users’
  poses and surrounding environments. Through an initial exploration and preliminary evaluation, our
  results demonstrate the potential of RL to position content in ways that maximize user reward
  during ongoing activity. We further outline future research directions for leveraging RL to enable
  personalized and adaptive UI placement in mixed reality systems.
